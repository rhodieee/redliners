The History of Redliners

Redliners was established early 1996, with the founder of Rodolfo Orbacedo and hes co-founder Rhodie Orbacedo; They started there business in the backyard shed of their house. Struggling to start a business they hosted car meets all around the Gold Coast getting there name around to people who are car enthusiast. There business was small then there name was floating around the city and suddnely bursted with fame. To this day Rhodie & Rodolfo have a successful business selling car performance parts and accessories for car people.

1996 - 1999

Redliners were basically a laughing stock compared to other car shops like supercheapautos and autobarn. Selling second-hand parts to car people and only having a limiting amount of stock for there JDM wreckings.

2000 - 2005

Redliners hosted events such as car meets with a few hundreds of cars attending and the company provided stickers which were free in increase the companys name, which helped them gained an outstanding amount of customers.

2005 - 2010

With Redliners business growing, they decided to hire a few specialist to increase there businesses amount of work they can do, including new products for car tuning, mechanics to fix problems of a car, car elecricians and a parts specialist who knows what needs suit you the most.

2010 - 2016

Redliners today is a successful business that was started out in a backyard with two amatures who thought there business would go no where. They are currently up to date with the latest technology and are very popular in the Gold Coast area wih the car scene and as of today they still hold events every Friday night for all the car enthusiast to meet up and show what progress they make with there cars!
